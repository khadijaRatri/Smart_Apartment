Security

Internet of Things (IoT) devices such as smart cameras and thermostats can be vulnerable to cyber atacks. Secure them by changing default passwords, keeping firmware updated, and segmenting them on a separate network if possible. Physical Security: Ensure physical security of your router and other network devices. Place them in a secure location to prevent unauthorized access or tampering.
